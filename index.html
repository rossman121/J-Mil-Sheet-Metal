What We Do

Commercial Sheet Metal Services

Fabricated to your exact specs — from standard duct runs to complex custom fittings. We handle the shop work so you can focus on the install.

Custom Duct Fabrication

Square duct, spiral pipe, transitions, and fittings fabricated to your project specs. S & drive, TDC, and weld flange connections available.

  • 4-foot joint square duct
  • Spiral pipe 6"–36" diameter
  • Transitions & offsets
  • Elbows & reducers

Grease Duct

16–18 gauge mild steel grease duct for commercial kitchen exhaust systems. Manufactured offsets, fittings, and elbows built to code.

  • Grease duct offsets & fittings
  • 16–18 gauge mild steel
  • Manufactured elbows
  • Custom configurations

Curb Adapters

Custom-dimensioned curb adapters fabricated from your existing curb measurements. Accurate fit every time, built to match rooftop unit requirements.

  • Custom dimensions on request
  • Fabricated from curb specs
  • Commercial RTU compatible
  • Fast production time

Plasma Cutting & Custom Fabrication

Cybermation HVAC plasma cutting system for precision custom parts, panels, and specialty components — including custom metal signage.

  • Cybermation plasma cutter
  • Precision HVAC components
  • Custom metal panels
  • Specialty fabrication
