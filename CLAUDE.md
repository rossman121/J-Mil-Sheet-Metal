## Design System

### Brand Colors
- **Primary Color:** Royal Blue (#1E5BA8 or similar - from J MIL signage)
- **Secondary Color:** Brown (#8B6F47 or similar - accent from contact section)
- **Accent Colors:** Use brown for highlights, CTAs, and section headers
- **Text:** Dark blue or charcoal on light backgrounds; white/light gray on dark backgrounds
- **Backgrounds:** White, light gray, or soft blue for sections

### Design Principles
1. **Professional & Clean** - Modern corporate aesthetic that appeals to B2B commercial clients
2. **Ease of Use** - Crystal clear navigation, intuitive layout, obvious CTAs
3. **Blue Collar Credible** - Showcase craftsmanship and precision; avoid overly trendy or frivolous design
4. **Technical Confidence** - Convey expertise through clean layouts and quality typography
5. **Fast & Scannable** - Contractors are busy; make key info immediately visible

### Typography
- Use clean, modern sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Roboto, Poppins)
- Hierarchy: Large headers for sections, clear subheaders, readable body text
- Ensure excellent contrast for readability

## Website Structure

### Pages Required
1. **Home / Hero Section**
   - Clear headline about J-Mil's value
   - Subheadline about custom duct fabrication
   - Call-to-action button: "Request a Quote" or "Contact Us"
   - Professional imagery of fabrication work (use placeholder if needed initially)

2. **Services Section**
   - Highlight key services:
     - Custom duct fabrication
     - Fast turnaround times
     - Commercial expertise
     - Quality welding
   - Use icons or brief descriptions
   - Simple, scannable format

3. **Portfolio Section**
   - Showcase completed projects (gallery/grid format)
   - Feature all service types with images:
     - Square Duct (4-foot joints, S and drive connections, TDC connection)
     - Square Transitions (center line, offsets, flat on top/bottom, elbows)
     - Spiral Pipe (6" to 36" diameter, up to 10' lengths)
     - Spiral Fittings (reducers, taps, elbows, caps, couplings)
     - Grease Duct (16-18 mild steel, manufactured offsets, fittings, elbows)
     - Stainless Steel Duct (16-18 gauge, 1/2" weld flanges, offsets, fittings)
     - Curb Adapters (custom dimensions from existing curb)
     - Plasma Cutter (Cybermation HVAC cutting system, custom signs)
   - Demonstrate range and quality of work
   - Should look professional and impressive

4. **Contact Section**
   - Contact form for quote requests
   - Company contact information
   - Clear CTA: "Get Your Quote Today"
   - Professional footer with navigation

## Development Instructions

### Session Start Checklist
- Always invoke the **front-end design skill** before writing any front-end code
- Review brand colors and design principles above
- Remember: Ease of use is critical - navigation must be intuitive
- Target audience: Busy HVAC contractors and builders - respect their time

### Important Design Rules
1. **Navigation:** Keep it simple - 4-5 main nav items max (Home, Services, Portfolio, Contact)
2. **CTAs:** Make quote/contact buttons prominent and obvious - use Brown (#8B6F47) for CTA buttons
3. **Colors:** Use Royal Blue (#1E5BA8) as primary and Brown (#8B6F47) as accent throughout for brand consistency
4. **Whitespace:** Don't crowd content - professional spacing is key
5. **Mobile First:** Ensure excellent mobile experience for contractors on job sites
6. **Show the Work:** Feature high-quality images of actual duct work and fabrication equipment to showcase craftsmanship

### Screenshot Loop Setup
- Enable Puppeteer for taking screenshots during development
- Use screenshots to compare against design references
- Take at least 2 passes of screenshots to polish design
- Screenshot locations: hero section, services, portfolio, contact/footer
